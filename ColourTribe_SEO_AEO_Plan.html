Executive Summary

This document describes the full SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) and AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) strategy implemented on the Colour Tribe website (colourtribe.co.in). The goal is twofold:

🔍 SEO — Rank Higher

Ensure every page on the site is technically optimised to appear in the top results when B2B buyers search for uniform manufacturers, hotel uniform suppliers, chef wear, and related queries on Google.

🤖 AEO — Get Cited by AI

Ensure Colour Tribe's answers appear in AI-powered search results (Google AI Mode, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) when buyers ask questions like "best uniform manufacturer in India" or "where to buy chef uniforms in bulk".

Target Market: Indian B2B buyers — hotel chains, restaurant groups, hospitals, corporate HR teams, and industrial firms — procuring uniforms in bulk (10–1000+ pieces).
02

What Was Implemented

A. index.html — Base-Layer SEO

  • Descriptive keyword-rich <title> tag targeting "B2B Uniform Manufacturer India"
  • 180-character <meta name="description"> with primary keywords + USPs
  • <meta name="keywords"> covering chef uniform, hotel uniform, corporate workwear, housekeeping, Greater Noida
  • <meta name="robots" content="index, follow">
  • <link rel="canonical"> pointing to the production domain
  • Full Open Graph tags (og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url, og:type)
  • Twitter Card meta tags (summary_large_image)
  • Colour Tribe logo set as favicon + Apple Touch Icon
  • <meta name="theme-color"> set to navy (#0f1b2d)

B. JSON-LD Structured Data (Static in HTML)

  • Organization schema — name, URL, logo, telephone, address, areaServed
  • ClothingStore / LocalBusiness schema — includes opening hours, priceRange, full address, telephone, description
Why JSON-LD? Structured data allows Google and AI engines to understand who Colour Tribe is without reading prose — dramatically increasing the chance of earning a Knowledge Panel, Rich Result, or AI citation.

04

JSON-LD Schema Strategy

Each schema type targets a specific Google Rich Result format and AI citation opportunity:

Schema Type Injected On Google Rich Result AEO Benefit
Organization All pages (static in HTML) Knowledge Panel, Sitelinks AI engines identify brand entity
ClothingStore / LocalBusiness All pages (static in HTML) Local Pack, Maps listing boost AI can cite business hours, address, phone
Product Every /product/:sku page Product Rich Snippet (name, price, availability) AI engines can cite specific product specs
FAQPage Homepage, About, Contact FAQ Accordion in search results Highest AEO value — direct AI answer sourcing
BreadcrumbList Catalogue + Product pages Breadcrumb trail in search snippet Improves page hierarchy understanding
Critical Insight — FAQPage Schema: When an AI engine (Google Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity) is asked "what is the minimum order for uniforms in India?", the presence of a FAQPage schema with that exact Q&A makes Colour Tribe a primary citation candidate.
05

AEO — Answer Engine Optimisation

AEO focuses on ensuring Colour Tribe's content is the authoritative, cited answer when buyers use AI-powered search tools. We implemented two layers:

Layer 1: FAQPage JSON-LD (Machine-Readable)

Injected invisibly in <head> on Homepage, About, and Contact pages. AI engines read this directly.

Layer 2: Visible FAQ Accordions (Human + Machine)

Native HTML <details><summary> accordions added visibly to About and Contact pages — readable by both crawlers and users.

PageFAQ Questions Targeting
Homepage Minimum order? Custom logo? Delivery time? Which industries? Pan-India delivery? What fabric?
About Where located? How many years experience? Manufacturer or reseller?
Contact How to place bulk order? Business hours? Sample policy? WhatsApp orders?
Homepage AEO: The 6 FAQ items on the homepage are also rendered as screen-reader-accessible aria-hidden="false" HTML, ensuring AI crawlers can parse the answers even if JavaScript is slow to execute.

Recommended Next Steps

🗺️ Submit Sitemap to Google

Generate a sitemap.xml listing all routes and submit via Google Search Console to trigger fast indexing of all new URL hubs.

📍 Google Business Profile

Claim and optimise the Google Business Profile for Colour Tribe at Greater Noida. Add photos, categories ("Clothing Manufacturer"), and link to the website.

🔗 Backlink Building

List Colour Tribe on JustDial, IndiaMART, Sulekha, TradeIndia, and LinkedIn. These backlinks signal authority to Google and AI crawlers.

📸 Real Product Images

Replace emoji placeholders with real product photos. Product Image schema requires an imageUrl. Actual photos dramatically improve click-through rates from search.

✍️ Blog / Content Hub

Add a blog at /blog with articles like "How to choose hotel uniforms", "5 benefits of polyviscose for workwear". Long-form content drives AEO citations.

⭐ Review Schema

Collect Google reviews from B2B clients and add AggregateRating schema to the homepage for visible star ratings in search results.

Priority: Steps 1 (Sitemap) and 2 (Google Business Profile) are free and can be completed in under 30 minutes each. They are the single highest-ROI actions after this technical implementation.
